How Pasa-Points Circulation Works

Your Active Wallet is capped at 2,500 pts — this is the balance used for all swaps. Any points earned beyond the cap automatically overflow into your Circulation Buffer. Whenever your active balance drops to 500 pts or below, the buffer automatically tops your active wallet back up to 2,500 — pulling from the soonest-to-expire entries first. Buffer points expire 30 days after they're added, so make sure to keep swapping!
